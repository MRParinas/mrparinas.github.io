Mark Parinas is a visionary leader whose career spans both the healthcare and software development industries. With over two decades of leadership experience, Parinas has a proven track record of leveraging technology to enhance healthcare delivery and improve patient outcomes, as well as funding software solutions that foster efficiency and innovation in clinical and business operations.

Parinas's journey began in the medical field, where his roles ranged from ICU and CCU Registered Nurse to a pivotal position at Kaiser Permanente and Sutter Healthcare, laying the groundwork for his deep understanding of healthcare's complexities. Transitioning into the realm of entrepreneurship, he founded two companies, CVHCare and Curaesoft. Curaesoft Corporation, is a software development firm focused on addressing the urgent needs within healthcare through agile methodologies and user-centered design principles. Under his tenure as Chairman, CVHCare and Curaesoft have developed software solutions that significantly enhance operational efficiency and clinical outcomes.

His commitment to community and healthcare advancement is evident through his active involvement with several boards and foundations, including the Masonic Homes of California Foundation, the Northern California Scholarships Foundation, Community Child Care Centers, and Shriners Hospitals for Children, where he has held roles from Trustee to Chairman of various committees, significantly contributing to their missions and impact.

Parinas embodies a leadership style marked by innovation, accountability, and a deep commitment to service. He champions a family-centric approach in healthcare, emphasizing the importance of enabling independence and quality of life for patients, particularly the elderly. In the business sphere, he is an advocate for remote work capabilities, recognizing and harnessing the transformative power of technology to reshape workplace dynamics for enhanced productivity and engagement.

Parinas's skills bridge the critical intersection of healthcare and technology, with specialties in clinical program development, business design, and strategic planning. His interests lie in chronic disease management, home-based care solutions, and leveraging emerging technologies to advance healthcare delivery. He also holds a keen interest in enhancing employee efficiency and accountability through innovative software solutions.

Mark Parinas's commitment to lifelong learning is reflected in his extensive academic achievements and certifications. He accomplished his clinical studies from the University of Santo Tomas, augmented by specialized certifications in ICU and CCU care with Kaiser Permanente and Sutter Health. Further expanding his expertise, he pursued graduate studies at New York University, alongside executive education from prestigious institutions like MIT, Wharton School, and Harvard University, covering areas from software development to general management and healthcare executive leadership.

Parinas's portfolio is enhanced by his active licensure as a Registered Nurse and Public Health Nurse, in addition to his board certification as a Fellow with the American College of Healthcare Executives (ACHE).

Mark Parinas is a multidimensional leader whose vast experience, innovative mindset, and dedication to enhancing healthcare and business processes through technology make him a valuable asset to any board of directors. His leadership is characterized by a forward-thinking approach, a commitment to excellence, and a deep-seated belief in the power of technology to transform lives and industries.
